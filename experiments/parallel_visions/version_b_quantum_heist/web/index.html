Version B · Quantum Relay Heist

Design a superposition robbery that plays out across overlapping timelines.

Game Idea

Core Fantasy: Assemble a crew of entangled specialists to break into a reality-shifting vault.

Gameplay Loop: Plan phases → assign crew to quantum tiles → resolve paradox waves → extract relic.

Audience Appeal: Fans of Into the Breach / Invisible Inc. who want crunchy tactics plus spectacle.

Suitability & Feasibility

  • Technical Scope: Hex/tile tactics grid, deterministic simulation, WebAssembly optional.
  • Festival Fit: Stylish heist UI, quick restarts, shareable "perfect run" seeds.
  • Risk Areas: Communicating quantum state, UI clarity in chaotic overlays.

Timeline Overlay Planner
