Aurora Loom — Ambient Bloomcraft

Sketch ribbons of light across an endless canopy. Each gesture seeds a bioluminescent bloom that drifts, harmonizing hue and tempo.

Prototype Goal: Validate tactile delight of weaving auroral threads with minimal UI.

Controls: Click / drag to paint, scroll to shift palette mood, double-tap to clear.

Future extensions: audio reactivity + saveable constellations.
